I wish it could be real…

Under a sky that forgets its stars, I’d borrow a little moonlight, just enough to trace your smile.

I’d ask the night to slow its breathing, so our steps could learn the language of soft and near.

Your hand in mine, and the world gentle, smaller like a secret we both promised to keep.

Every turn a quiet vow, every sway a yes, every heartbeat a lantern lit between us.

If wishes had feet, they’d find you; until the morning forgives the dream.
